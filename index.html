FAQ

Is SkillSync a job board?

No. SkillSync is a recruiter-guided platform designed to help early-career CPG professionals get visibility and better-fit matching—without endless applying.

Do you guarantee placement?

No. Outcomes depend on fit and active openings. The goal is to improve your visibility, alignment, and consideration for roles.

Why not collect resumes now?

We keep signup low-friction. Once you’re selected for the next phase, we’ll invite you to complete your profile and resume intake.

What info do you collect today?

Basic contact details and a few quick signals about your experience and goals. Nothing heavy.
